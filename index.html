FlappyType or TypingBird..

Anyway.. I enjoy playing typing Games and Playing flappyBird so I combined the two. More info on the game on my GitHub.Maybe i'll add leaderboards and logins oneday, For now just have fun with the beta ;)

Rules

  1. Normal Flappy Bird rules, dont hit the pole! Spacebar to jump
  2. Typing rules , Type the word before it passes the bird
  3. Spaces before and between words will not be counted :)
  4. Points are awarded for each word typed correctly
